The Barioth is a Flying Wyvern that roams the frozen Tundra of Monster Hunter Rise. The Tundra is a difficult area for many monsters to traverse as many of the monster in Monster Hunter World do not have the anatomical advantages the Barioth possesses. The strong forelegs, tail, and spiked scales make navigating slippery surfaces easy and aids the Barioth's hunting for other monsters that are not so accustomed to the frozen Tundra. The Barioth is a very dangerous creature that will use its wings and spikes to move at incredibly fast speeds. Every Hunter looking to take down this Wyvern should be targeting those points to reduce the Barioth's great speed when on ice. Without speed, the Barioth is not a difficult hunt.
