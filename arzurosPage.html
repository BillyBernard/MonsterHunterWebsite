Arzuros


Large Monsters Home Page


The Arzuros is a Fanged Beast who enjoys the humid forests and mountainous regions of Monster Hunter Rise. These monsters can be seen in the wild fishing or using their strong hind legs to stand upright and gather honey for food. If threatened an Arzuros will use its thick claws and heavy forearm plating to fight off any threat in its path. Due their love of honey, the Arzuros will resort to stealing honey that hunters have within their inventory. This is both a blessing and a curse to the Arzuros. The monster gets its much loved hiney but will become distracted leaving a easy opening for hunters to strike. The seasoned hunter will bring honey and use this exploit against any targeted Arzuros.
